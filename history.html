Hello and welcome to Waterford City!

Waterford is a vibrant city located in the southeast of Ireland, known for its rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and bustling cultural scene. It is home to numerous attractions, including the famous Waterford Crystal factory, the Medieval Museum, and the scenic Waterford Greenway. The city also offers a wide range of sports and outdoor activities, such as kayaking, hiking, and golfing, making it an ideal destination for adventure seekers. With its friendly locals, lively nightlife, and delicious cuisine, Waterford is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the best of Ireland.
